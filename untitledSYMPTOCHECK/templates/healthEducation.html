Health Education Highlights

Common Cold vs Flu
Colds usually cause mild congestion and sore throat. Flu often brings high fever and body aches. Symptom duration and severity help differentiate them.
Managing Fever at Home
Stay hydrated, rest, and use paracetamol if needed. Seek care for persistent high fever, dehydration, or altered consciousness.
When to Test
If symptoms worsen, are very specific to a disease (loss of smell/taste, rash), or you belong to a high-risk group, testing is advised.
Mental Health First Steps
Check-in with your mood and sleep. Use grounding techniques, reach out to someone you trust, and seek professional support if needed.
