Safety Measures & Guidelines

  • Keep emergency numbers accessible.
  • Learn basic first aid and CPR.
  • Keep a first-aid kit at home and in vehicles.
  • Follow evacuation plans in case of disasters.
  • Use caution and call emergency services in accidents.
  • Prepare emergency supplies: water, food, torch, medications.
  • Inform family/friends of your location in emergencies.
