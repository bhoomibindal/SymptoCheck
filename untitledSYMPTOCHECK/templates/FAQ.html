Quick Answers

What is this health portal about?
This portal helps you track your wellbeing, manage medications, record doctor notes, and access emergency resources — all in one place.
How do I track my health daily?
Go to the Health Tracker page, where you can log exercise, food, mood, and get summaries of your progress.
Can I add reminders for medications?
Yes, the Medication Tracker lets you log your medicines and schedule reminders for doses.
Is my data private?
Your information is stored securely in your browser (localStorage) for this demo. For real apps, secure cloud databases with encryption are used.
Can I access this on mobile?
Yes! The website is designed to be fully responsive and mobile-friendly.
